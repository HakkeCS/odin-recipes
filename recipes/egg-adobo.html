Chipotle Adobo Pickled Eggs


Description

These pickled eggs are great sliced and served on a salad or just eat them plain for a quick low carb snack! You can adjust the heat by adding more or less chipotle and adobo.


Ingredients

  • White Vinegar
  • Water
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Chipotle Chilies
  • Adobo Sauce
  • Eggs

Steps


  1. Add ingredients to sealable jars
  2. Let sit in cool dark place for at least 1 day up to 2 weeks
